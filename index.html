  • Automatic Facial Recognition Door

    Circuit Analysis, RaspberryPi, C

    I collaborated with two engineering students as a sub-team to create (from scratch) the mechanical system used in this project. This involved designing and testing a circuit that would deliver signals to an electric motor and electric strike (lock) for opening the door when facial recognition was successful. We were able to complete a working prototype.

  • Pedstrian Crossing Project

    Arduino, C Programming Language, Circuit Design

    I created a pedestrian crossing where the pedestrian pushes the "walk" button. This signals the traffic light that there's a person wanting to walk. The traffic light turns red and the pedestrian light turns green while an LCD has a countdown for the pedestrian.
